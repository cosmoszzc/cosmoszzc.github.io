About

Who I am

I am a PhD candidate in Electrical Engineering at Universiti Malaya, working on post-operative longitudinal glioma segmentation with deep learning. My path started in mechanical engineering: I designed laser engravers, quick-opening loading boxes, and CAM linkage fixtures before I ever wrote a line of Python. That hands-on instinct still drives how I approach AI research — I care about models that actually work on real data, not just on leaderboards.
